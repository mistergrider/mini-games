SCOUNDREL — RULES

The Deck (44 cards)

♣ Clubs & ♠ Spades (2–10, J, Q, K, A) are MONSTERS. Face values: J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=14.

♦ Diamonds (2–10) are WEAPONS. ♥ Hearts (2–10) are POTIONS.

Goal

Clear the entire dungeon deck without your HP dropping to 0. You start at 20/20 HP.

The Room

Each room is 4 face-up cards. On your turn you must resolve 3 of the 4, in any order you like. The 1 card left over carries forward and joins 3 freshly drawn cards to form the next room. Once the deck runs dry, you must resolve everything that's left.

Fighting a Monster

Fight it barehanded — lose HP equal to its full value — or fight it with your equipped weapon — lose HP equal to (monster value − weapon value), minimum 0.

Using a weapon stacks that monster underneath it. After that, the weapon can only be used again on a monster of equal or lesser value than the last one it defeated — it dulls with use.

Equipping a Weapon

Equip it immediately. Any previously equipped weapon — and every monster stacked beneath it — is discarded.

Drinking a Potion

Heal HP equal to its value, capped at your 20 max. Only the first potion resolved in a room heals you — any additional potions that same room are wasted.

Fleeing a Room

Instead of resolving any cards, flee: all 4 room cards go to the bottom of the deck (in order) and you draw a fresh room. You can't flee two rooms in a row, and you can't flee once you've already resolved a card in the current room.

Winning & Scoring

Clear the dungeon and your score equals your remaining HP. If you finish at a full 20 HP and the very last card you resolved was a potion, add that potion's value as a bonus.

Losing & Scoring

If your HP hits 0, the run ends immediately. Your score is the negative sum of every monster's value still left in the dungeon (deck + current room).

The Decks

Tavern deck (40 cards): each suit ♠♥♦♣ has ranks 2–10 plus an Ace. Aces are Animal Companions, worth 1.

Castle deck (12 cards): the four Jacks (ATK 10, HP 20), then the four Queens (ATK 15, HP 30), then the four Kings (ATK 20, HP 40) — shuffled within each rank, and always fought in that order.

Goal

Defeat all 12 royals before your hand fails you. You draw a hand of 8 from the Tavern deck to begin; 8 is always your hand cap.

Your Turn

Play a single card, an Animal Companion paired with one other card (combined value = 1 + the other card's value, or 2 if pairing two Companions), or 2–4 cards of the same rank whose values sum to 10 or less — or YIELD to skip your attack entirely.

Suit Powers

Each suit present among the card(s) you play triggers once:

♥ Hearts — shuffle the discard pile, then move that many cards from it to the bottom of the Tavern deck.

♦ Diamonds — draw that many cards from the Tavern deck (hand cap 8).

♣ Clubs — the damage you deal this attack is doubled.

♠ Spades — reduces this enemy's attack by that amount for the rest of the fight (stacks across turns against the same enemy).

Immunity

Every royal is immune to its own suit's power — that suit's bonus effect is negated — but the attack's raw value still deals damage as normal.

Defeating an Enemy

Damage equal to your combo's value is dealt. If the enemy's HP hits exactly 0, it's an exact strike — the royal is placed face-down on top of the Tavern deck, so you'll draw it again soon. Any overkill sends it to the discard pile instead. Either way, your played cards go to the discard pile and the next royal is revealed.

The Enemy Strikes Back

If the enemy survives your attack, it strikes back for (its ATK − any accumulated Spade shield). You must discard cards from your hand totalling at least that amount to survive. If your entire hand's value can't cover it, you fall immediately.

The Jester

Twice per game you may flip a Jester: discard your whole hand and draw a fresh 8. Use it before attacking, or before discarding to survive a strike.

Winning & Rank

Defeat all 12 royals to win. Your rank depends on how many Jesters you used: 0 = GOLD, 1 = SILVER, 2 = BRONZE.
